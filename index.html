Delete Your Account

You can request permanent deletion of your account and associated data for the WIMB App.

How to request account deletion?

  • Open the Emunsi App.
  • Go to the Settings section.
  • Tap on Delete Profile.
  • Confirm to permanently delete your account and data.

What data will be deleted?

  • Profile Information (Name, Mobile, Email)
  • Business Details (Turnover, Work Area, Experience)
  • Tender Preferences (State/District selections)

What data may be retained?

Certain records may be retained for legal or compliance purposes (e.g., billing or financial transactions), but will not be used for any other purpose.
